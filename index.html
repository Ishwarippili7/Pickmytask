About Us

EarnPe - Turn Your Time Into Money

Our Story

EarnPe was launched in 2023 with the belief that everyone should be able to earn money in their free time. We provide a platform for users to earn income by completing simple tasks for various companies and brands.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide earning opportunities to every Indian, especially students, homemakers, and part-time workers to generate additional income in their spare time.

Our Values

  • Transparency: All task details and payments are clear
  • Trust: Secure payments and data privacy
  • Reliability: Timely payments and support
  • Innovation: Continuously introducing new earning opportunities

Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your data

Data Collection

We only collect information necessary to create your account and provide our services:

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Payment details (UPI ID or bank account details)
  • Task completion time and status

Data Security

We use various security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL)
  • Secure Firebase database
  • Regular security audits

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Legal requirements
  • Providing services (payment processing)
  • With your consent

Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Correct your data
  • Delete your account
  • Object to data sharing
